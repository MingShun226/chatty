## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Shipping & Delivery

**Q: Do you deliver nationwide?**
A: Yes, we deliver to all states in Malaysia including Sabah and Sarawak.

**Q: How long does delivery take?**
A:
- Klang Valley: 1-2 working days
- West Malaysia: 2-4 working days
- East Malaysia: 3-6 working days

**Q: What is the delivery fee?**
A:
- Orders above RM200: FREE delivery
- Orders below RM200: RM15 delivery fee
- Sabah & Sarawak: RM30 delivery fee

**Q: Can I track my order?**
A: Yes, you will receive a tracking number via SMS and email once your order is shipped.

### Payment Methods

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A:
- Online banking (FPX)
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- E-wallets (GrabPay, Touch 'n Go, Boost)
- Cash on Delivery (COD) - selected areas only

A: Yes, our payment gateway is SSL encrypted and PCI-DSS compliant for maximum security.
